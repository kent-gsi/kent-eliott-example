as a Family Man

Discovering the other side of T.S. Eliot through the letters he wrote to his families.

T.S. Eliot as a Family Man is a digital humanities project which invites you to look at the other—less esoteric yet more amiable—side of the liteary giant. This is a database comprised of letters Eliot wrote to his families—his mother, his brother, and his wife.
